Designed for Maximum Productivity

Every feature is purpose-built to eliminate friction, enforce your intentions, and protect your most valuable asset — your attention.

Scheduled Focus Mode

Set a custom start and end time for your deep work sessions. During those hours, blocked sites redirect to a calming full-screen page. Midnight crossover is handled intelligently for night-shift workers.

Smart Auto-Grouping

Every tab is automatically analyzed and grouped by category in real time — silently, in the background. Open GitHub? Instantly snapped into "Development". Reddit? Lands in "Social Media", already collapsed.

Fully Customizable Categories

Ships with 4 default categories: Social Media, Video & Entertainment, Work/Development, and News. Rename, delete, or create new ones — add unlimited domains. Every change takes effect instantly.

Right-Click Quick-Add

Stumble onto a new time-sink? Simply right-click anywhere on the page and inject the current domain into any of your categories. Takes less than two seconds. Works on any website.

Forgiving Mode Bypasses

Need urgent access? A "Take a Breath" button triggers a 5-second guided breathing animation — making you pause before proceeding. Grants 1 minute of access, strictly limited to one bypass per 5-hour period per domain.

Distraction Shield Counter

A live counter tracks exactly how many times Focus Assistant has blocked a distraction today. Resets at midnight — your daily productivity scoreboard, giving you raw proof of how hard your shield is working.

In-Browser Alert Banners

Elegant bottom-right sliding notification banners fire when your focus session begins, when it's 5 minutes from ending (with a 30-minute extension option), and when a site is added. Paired with synthesized audio pings via the Web Audio API — zero external dependencies.

Dynamic Motivational Quotes

Every blocked page loads a freshly randomized motivational quote from a curated productivity-focused collection. Keeps the blocking experience positive and intentional rather than punishing.
