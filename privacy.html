Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At Focus Assistant, we believe your data belongs to you. Our extension is designed from the ground up to operate 100% locally on your device. We do not collect, store, sell, or transmit any personal data.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Focus Assistant requires certain permissions (such as reading tabs or host permissions) strictly to function as a productivity tool—specifically to block distracting websites and group your tabs.

We DO NOT:

  • Collect Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, emails, or IP addresses.
  • Send your browsing history, tab data, or settings to any external servers.
  • Track your activity using third-party analytics or telemetry.

3. Local Storage

All of your settings, schedules, custom categories, and usage statistics are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage utilizing the standard Extension API. If you uninstall the extension, this local data is completely removed from your device.

4. Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our extension or relevant regulations. However, our core philosophy—that your data never leaves your device—will not change.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us through our GitHub repository.
